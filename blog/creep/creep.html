Creep


The other day I was listening to Creep by Radiohead. The lyrics struck me with a sense of disappointment that so many would be feeling this way. The first verse and chorus go like this:

When you were here before, couldn't look you in the eye
You're just like an angel, your skin makes me cry
You float like a feather in a beautiful world
I wish I was special, you're so fuckin' special
But I'm a creep
I'm a weirdo
What the hell am I doin' here?
I don't belong here
